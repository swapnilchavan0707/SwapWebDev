The Invincible Record

Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj is one of the few kings in world history who remained undefeated in over 120 battles. His military career was a masterclass in speed, precision, and psychological warfare.

Major Campaigns

  • Battle of Burhanpur: A lightning-fast strike that shook the Mughal economy.
  • Siege of Janjira: A daring attempt to conquer the 'invincible' sea fort of the Siddis.
  • War with Portuguese: Defeated the Portuguese at Goa, showcasing his naval and land prowess.

The Shield of Swarajya

For nine long years, Sambhaji Maharaj stood as a wall between the massive Mughal Army of 500,000 soldiers and the Deccan. He protected the dream of his father, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, ensuring the flame of Swarajya never dimmed.
